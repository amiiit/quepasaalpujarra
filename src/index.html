Que Pasa Alpujarra - the community calendar

There are so many things going on around here and many times we miss them. That's why we've decided to create a space where we can get information about concerts, workshops, parties or any other kind of event. We, as many others get a lot of valuable information from Órgiva Massive on facebook, but we've realized that those infos tend to get lost pretty quickly. So it would be great if people share any ongoing events here in addition to their publicity on social networks, to make sure everybody can find them easily. This is an initial edition of QuePasaAlpujarra and we hope that it's going to be used and that it contributes to the social and cultural life in our area. If you have any questions, comments or suggestions, please contact us over our email: contact@quepasaalpujarra.com

How to add an event?

Adding an event to our local events calendar is just like sending us a calendar invitation from your favorite calendar program to eventos@quepasaalpujarra.com. It doesn't matter what calendar you are using, whether it's a mac or android calendar, google or microsoft or any other kind of software. Most calendars people use today are supporting the iCal format which is a kind of invitation sent over email that computers can understand. If you are using any digital calendar, it's most probably using the iCal format. If any doubts just search the internet for "how to send a calendar invite".

Once the invitation arrived it should automatically apear on the public calendar within some minutes.

Do you think it's complicated?

We're aware of the fact that for some people doing this is not as straightforward and easy as for others. Can it be simplified? Sure, but it will require additional programming effort from our side. We are willing to do so but we'd like to see that this calendar is being used first, so it's worth the effort. You have ideas how to improve the process of adding event? Let us know over contact@quepasaalpujarra.com

Como añadir un evento?

Añadir un evento al calendario de eventos es igual que mandar una invitación a alguien sobre tu programa favorita que usas para administrar tu calendario personal. Lo que tienes que hacer es mandar una invitación a eventos@quepasaalpujarra.com con todos los detalles del evento. Es totalmente igual si usas un mac, android, iPhone, google, microsoft o cualquier tipo de calendario online. La mayoría de los calendarios usan el formato iCal. Si tienes dudas como hacerlo búsca por internet "Como mandar una invitación desde el calendario"

Cuándo tu invitatción ha llegado tendría que aparecer automaticamente en unos minutos.

Legal statement

Que pasa Alpujarra is a voluntary project initiated and run by volunteers with the only goal to allow people to publish their events and for others to find information about events. We are not affiliated with any of the event holder, nor we take any guarantee or responsibility to any of the events. If you feel that any of the information presented here is offending, malicious or faked please contact us and let us know. We reserve the right to remove events due to reasons we might, or might not formulate in the future. Removing events from this calendar is is something we try to avoid but we will do this if we feel that a certain event or a set of events ruins the calendar for everybody else.

Privacy

We respect the right for privacy of the visitors of this site and therefore collect no data at all from any one visiting this site. In the future we might implement a traffic counter to tell us how many people are actually using this site, but we'll do this without tracking our users. Needless to say we do not sell any data to no one because we don't simply don't collect any. With that being said we at the moment are still using google's implementation for managing the actual calendar, so we can't provide any guarantees to what google is doing and we're not responsible to any personal data that google might be tracking. For a safe and and private browsing we recommend using the ghostery plugin for all major browsers.
