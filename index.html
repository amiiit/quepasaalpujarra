Que Pasa Alpujarra - the community calendar

There's a lot going on here, so we've decided to create a place where people can get informed about what's going on in our area, publicly and without any need to log in anywhere. We, as many others are getting a lot of valuable information from Órgiva Massive on facebook and we see this page as an addition to the activity happening over there. What we also realized however, is that information about events tends to get lost in the feed and that it's really hard to find some post you saw last week about a concert happening in the coming weekend. So we ask people to post their event here additionally to their post on their favorite platforms, just to make sure that others can find this easily.

This is the initial edition of Que Pasa Alpujarra and we sure hope it's going to be used, as all feedback from people about this was very positive. We have many ideas how to improve this calendar and we intend to implement them as time allows and of course, if we see that this actually contributes to the social and cultural life here. If you got more ideas, or if you'd like to help please contact us over our email: contacto@quepasaalpujarra.com

How to add an event?

Adding an event to our local events calendar is just like sending us a calendar invitation from your favorite calendar program to eventos@quepasaalpujarra.com. It doesn't matter what calendar you are using, whether it's a mac or android calendar, google or microsoft or any other kind of software. Most calendars people use today are supporting the iCal format which is a kind of invitation sent over email that computers can understand. If you are using any digital calendar, it's most probably using the iCal format. If any doubts just search the internet for "how to send a calendar invite"

Once we receive the event in our mailbox we will accept it as soon as possible, so that people can see, and hopefully participate in your event.

Legal statement

Que pasa Alpujarra is a voluntary project initiated and run by volunteers with the only goal to allow people to publish their events and for others to find information about events. We are not affiliated with any of the event holder, nor we take any guarantee or responsibility to any of the events. If you feel that any of the information presented here is offending, malicious or faked please contact us and let us know. We reserve the right to remove events due to reasons we might, or might not formulate in the future. Removing events from this calendar is is something we try to avoid but we will do this if we feel that a certain event or a set of events ruins the calendar for everybody else.

Privacy

We respect the right for privacy of the visitors of this site and therefore collect no data at all from any one visiting this site. In the future we might implement a traffic counter to tell us how many people are actually using this site, but we'll do this without tracking our users. Needless to say we do not sell any data to no one because we don't simply don't collect any. With that being said we at the moment are still using google's implementation for managing the actual calendar, so we can't guarantee that not responsible to any personal data that google might be tracking. For a safe and and private browsing we recommend using the ghostery plugin for all major browsers.
